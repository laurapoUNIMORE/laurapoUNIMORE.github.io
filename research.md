# Research Interests

My research focuses on **data management, artificial intelligence, and urban data analytics**.

## Main Research Areas

### Big Data Management
Techniques and systems for managing large-scale datasets.

### Semantic Web and Linked Open Data
Integration and enrichment of heterogeneous datasets.

### Artificial Intelligence
Machine learning and natural language processing.

### Smart Cities and Urban Analytics
Data-driven analysis of urban mobility and environmental data.

### Digital Twin
Modeling and simulation of urban environments through data streams.
